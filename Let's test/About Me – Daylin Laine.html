About Me

Daylin Laine is a NYC based makeup artist with 10 years of experience in the industry with a focus on boudoir, bridal, and anything with a dark & vampy twist!

She began her career assisting on local Burlesque photoshoots. From there, she started taking on amateur shoots which eventually led a focus on wedding and editorial styles. In the bridal and boudoir world, she found her calling and has worked on editorials for Tartarus Magazine, Opera Magazine, and Vogue Italia, to name a few. She loves being able to explore all paths of makeup with her focus on making anyone and everyone who sits in her chair, feel beautiful in their natural skin. Her specialty remains boudoir with a focus on lgbtq couples.

Daylin works in the tristate area and out of her apartment in Astoria, NY with her partner and 3 cats! Working as a makeup artist gives her the creativity and passion needed in her life.
